# YouTube Content Strategy - Breaking the Loop

Session: [[21 Nov 2025]]

## Context

Voice note processing - raw frustration about not converting projects into shareable content, despite having expertise, insights, and communication skills.

**The core problem:** Over-optimizing workflows and systems instead of producing output. "How impressive your system is, not what you're doing with that system."

**The goal:** Credibility → Audience → Patronage → Ability to leave job (constrained by EU citizenship timeline)

## Key Insights

### The Pattern Identified
- Evaluating quality before finishing → nothing ships
- Starting new things while old things are still growing
- Sophisticated system-building as procrastination

### The Solution
Document actual work in public. Woodworking analogy: find a thing you want to make, document how you plan it, design it, problem-solve along the way, and eventually have a finished thing. The content is the byproduct, not the goal.

**The forcing function:** Weekly video deadlines create accountability to ship.

## Decision: Project Norma for YouTube

### Why Not Other Projects
- **youtube-ai-labels:** Don't care enough about it
- **expense-splitter:** Privacy concerns, larger scope, paused momentum
- **digital garden:** Too many layers to explain
- **bureaucracy-tool:** Haven't thought it through
- **Starting fresh:** Classic trap - more starting, less finishing

### Why Project Norma
- **Actually care about it** - kept coming back to it
- **Working prototype exists** (Nov 16) - 3 Python scripts, ~530 lines
- **110-year dictation history research** - compelling narrative hook
- **Universal pain point** - everyone's frustrated with Apple Voice Memos
- **"Intercom button" concept** - instantly understandable
- **Already dogfooding** - real usage validates it works

### The Narrative Arc
Project Norma digitizes 110 years of proven dictation workflows using modern AI (Whisper + Claude). It's not trendy AI hype - it's evolution, not invention.

**Thesis in action:** "AI as shield against bureaucracy" = using AI to automate the friction between your brain and captured thoughts.
